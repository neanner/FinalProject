'Ello! my name is Jeanna and I am learning how to take pictures! Last Christmas, I received my first DSLR camera. A few months later, my first friend engagement happened and I volunteered myself to take photos for her to use for her save the dates. From there, I started to get requests from other friends one by one. My approach to photography is to let the subjects that I work with be themselves and interact with the camera however they feel the most comfortable. I enjoy picking the setting of the photograph and letting the subject choose how they would like to pose. While I still have a long way to go in terms of technical precision, I've found that some of the best photos I have seen capture the essence of a moment instead of being just a pristine shot. What I aim for in any photoshoot that I do, is to find those moments that reflect the subject's personality and capture them as best as I can. People first, always.
